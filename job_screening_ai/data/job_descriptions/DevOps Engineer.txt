 **DevOps Engineer**

**About Us:**  
TechCorp is a cutting-edge software development company headquartered in San Francisco, CA. We are committed to delivering scalable, reliable, and secure digital solutions across cloud-native platforms and enterprise ecosystems.

---

**Job Description:**  
We are seeking a passionate and skilled **DevOps Engineer** to join our Infrastructure & Automation team. The ideal candidate will play a key role in optimizing development pipelines, automating infrastructure, and ensuring system reliability at scale.

---

**Responsibilities:**
- Design, implement, and maintain CI/CD pipelines using tools like Jenkins, GitHub Actions, or GitLab CI  
- Automate infrastructure provisioning using Infrastructure-as-Code tools (Terraform, CloudFormation)  
- Monitor system performance and ensure high availability of services using observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK stack)  
- Collaborate with developers to streamline application deployments and improve software delivery speed  
- Manage and optimize Kubernetes clusters and Docker containers in production environments  
- Implement robust security, backup, and disaster recovery strategies  
- Troubleshoot production issues and perform root cause analysis  

---

**Requirements:**
- 3+ years of experience in a DevOps, Site Reliability, or Systems Engineering role  
- Proficiency with CI/CD tools and automation scripting (Bash, Python, or Groovy)  
- Hands-on experience with Docker and container orchestration using Kubernetes  
- Solid understanding of Linux/Unix system administration  
- Experience with cloud platforms such as AWS, GCP, or Azure  
- Familiarity with version control systems (Git) and agile software development workflows  

---

**Preferred Skills:**
- Experience with configuration management tools like Ansible, Chef, or Puppet  
- Knowledge of service mesh technologies (Istio, Linkerd)  
- Experience with secrets management tools (Vault, AWS Secrets Manager)  
- Understanding of Zero Downtime Deployments and Blue/Green or Canary release strategies  
- Cloud certification (e.g., AWS Certified DevOps Engineer, CKA/CKAD)

---

**Education:**  
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related field
